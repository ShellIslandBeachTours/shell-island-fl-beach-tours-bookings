Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if the weather is bad on the day of my tour?

In the event of bad weather, we may need to reschedule or cancel your tour. Rest assured, we'll keep you informed every step of the way and work with you to find a suitable solution.

Can I bring food or beverages on the tour?

You're welcome to bring food and beverages on the tour, but please be mindful of any waste and dispose of it properly to help preserve the island's natural beauty.

How long are the standard tours?

The duration of the standard tours varies depending on the package you choose. Full-day tours typically last around 6-8 hours, while half-day tours last approximately 3-4 hours.

What tour options are available at Shell Island Beach Tours?

We offer a variety of tour options, including full-day tours, half-day tours, snorkeling excursions, and private charters.

What safety measures are in place to protect guests during the tours?

We have implemented enhanced safety measures to protect our guests during the tours, including increased cleaning and sanitization, reduced tour capacities, and adherence to strict safety protocols by our experienced guides.
